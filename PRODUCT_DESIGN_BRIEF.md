# PRODUCT DESIGN BRIEF — Dawayir

## Project Context
- Project: Dawayir (Live Cognitive OS)
- User: Knowledge worker under cognitive/emotional pressure
- Industry: Mental wellness + reflective AI
- Success Goal: Increase actionable clarity per session and drive measurable return usage

## STEP 1 — Job To Be Done
"The user wants to convert cognitive overload into one actionable decision within 10 minutes when they are mentally fragmented, so they do not default to unstructured rumination or endless venting."

## STEP 2 — Binding Decisions
1. What must be clear in 2 seconds without explanation?
- This is not a chatbot. It is a live cognitive mirror (3 circles changing in real-time).

2. What must not exist at all?
- Generic advice blocks, heavy clinical jargon, cluttered UI, long onboarding before first value.

3. What moment defines product identity emotionally?
- The first "Moment of No Return": visible shift + immediate "Why now" explanation.

4. What is first-use value?
- One concrete, personalized Truth Contract action the user can execute today.

5. What should the user feel when leaving?
- Seen, clear, and ready to act (not just emotionally soothed).

## STEP 3 — Single Persona
- Name / age / context:
  - Karim, 31, Product Manager, high interruption workload, decision fatigue.

- Biggest frustration with existing solutions:
  - Existing apps are either generic chat, slow journaling, or abstract advice with low execution value.

- "I wish there was..."
  - "A tool that listens in real-time and gives me one precise next step immediately."

- Measurable satisfaction behavior:
  - Completes 3 sessions within 7 days and marks Truth Contract as Done at least once within 24 hours.

## STEP 4 — Success Criterion
"We consider design successful when the user completes Truth Contract and returns for a second session within 24 hours after session end."

## STEP 5 — Anti-Goals
1. Becoming a knowledge-heavy psychoeducation app.
- Why: Increases cognitive load and weakens real-time clarity.

2. Optimizing for session length instead of decision quality.
- Why: Outcome is fast clarity + execution, not time spent.

3. Supporting multiple personas in V1.
- Why: Diffuses product focus before clear PMF with one core user.

## Product Principles (Operational)
- Clarity over complexity.
- Intervention over conversation volume.
- Behavior change over sentiment score.
- One-session value + cross-session memory loop.

## Core Metrics Dashboard (V1)
- Truth Contract completion rate (24h)
- Moment of No Return per session
- 7-day relapse risk trend
- Session return rate (D1 / D7)
- Reminder outcomes (Done vs Snooze)
