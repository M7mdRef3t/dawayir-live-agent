- start the live interaction

- keep transcript in frame

## 00:40 - 00:55

- keep `Why Now` visible if possible
- avoid cuts during the strongest motion

## 00:55 - 01:10

## 01:55 - 02:10

## 02:10 - 02:20

## 02:50 - 03:05

## 03:05 - 03:20

## 03:20 - 03:35

## 03:35 - 03:50

- hold the cleanest strongest judge frame
- no cursor movement

## 03:50 - 04:00

- end on the strongest final visual
- cut cleanly on the final line

## Recording Notes

- use one continuous screen recording when possible
- avoid random mouse movement
- do not zoom unless a detail is unreadable
- keep subtitles short and centered low enough to avoid UI overlap
- if a live step feels risky, use the prepared screenshot or replay artifact instead
